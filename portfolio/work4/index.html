Urban GIS: Mapping Early Childhood Education Access

My Urban GIS class produced an analysis of city-wide early education access in partnership with the Saint Paul city council.

Our class partnered with City Councilmember Rebecca Noecker in support of the proposed Saint Paul 3K Initiative. We created a series of maps identifying areas of demographic need and potential barriers to program access.

Check out our ArcGIS Story Map

As well as our interactive program-mapping app

It was exciting to produce maps that contributed to ongoing policy efforts.

This project was my first opportunity to get familiar with ArcGIS Pro, as well as an opportunity to continue building on my experience with ArcGIS Story Maps and web apps.
