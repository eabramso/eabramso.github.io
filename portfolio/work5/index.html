Climate Vulnerability and Resilience Mapping

Contributing to the creation of these ArcGIS Story Maps was a highlight of my internship with the Metropolitan Council.

In summer and fall 2018, I had the opportunity to work on the Metropolitan Council’s award-winning Climate Vulnerability Assessment. Working with Eric Wojchik, Ally de Alcuaz, and Meghan Klapper, I contributed to the creation of two ArcGIS Story Maps:

One about Extreme Heat in the Twin Cities Metro, https://metrocouncil.maps.arcgis.com/apps/MapJournal/index.html?appid=7d9cdd3929e9439bb5b25aa1186d5783

And one about Localized Flooding in the Twin Cities Metro. https://metrocouncil.maps.arcgis.com/apps/MapJournal/index.html?appid=fc03cf3e0f5a4a748811e8296cc690ee
