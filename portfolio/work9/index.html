Interactive Dashboards

As part of my internship with the Great Plains Institute, I’ve had the opportunity to create multiple interactive online maps.

Using ArcGIS Online, I created an interactive web map displaying solar and energy efficiency projects funded by the City of Minneapolis as part of their Green Business Cost Share program. This program helps businesses go solar, reduce air pollution, and improve energy efficiency. The program focuses especially on environmental justice areas in the city.

Here’s the link to the interactive map I created to display outcomes of this program’s 2018 array of projects.

I also used ArcGIS Online to update an existing map of electric vehicle (EV) registration and convert it to an interactive format. This map displays electric vehicle deployment throughout the state of Minnesota and highlights both existing and future EV charging corridors.

Here’s the link to the map of EV registration in Minnesota.

Another interactive map, focused on informing legislative strategy on Electric Vehicle readiness, is featured on the Drive Electric Minnesota website.
